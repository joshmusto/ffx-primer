Yojimbo

Location: Cavern of the Stolen Fayth

Damage Limit Unlock: Mars Sigil

Unlock Method:

Enter the Cavern of the Stolen Fayth, accessible after reaching the Calm Lands. Navigate the dungeon and defeat the boss Yojimbo at the end. Haggle with Yojimbo's fayth in the following way:

125,001 gil --> 148,051 gil --> 178,931 gil

Wait 5-8 seconds before entering an offer to prevent him from declining. (Optionally, give Yojimbo 3x his asking price at any point to hire him and receive two Teleport Spheres.)

Anima

Location: Baaj Temple

Damage Limit Unlock: None

Unlock Method:

Using the airship, fly to Baaj Temple. After defeating the boss Geosgaeno underwater, enter the Chamber of the Fayth. If all six destruction sphere puzzles have been solved in each Cloister of Trials, you will be able to claim Anima. If the Besaid and/or Macalania Temples have not been solved, you will need to defeat Dark Valefor and/or Dark Shiva respectively in order to access the temples.

Magus Sisters

Location: Remiem Temple

Damage Limit Unlock: None

Unlock Method:

Obtain all other Aeons. Obtain the Blossom Crown by capturing every monster on Mt. Gagazet and unlocking Catoblepas. Obtain the Flower Sceptre by defeating Belgemine's Bahamut.

Celestial Weapons

The Celestial Mirror is required to acquire some and power up all Celestial Weapons.

Some Celestial Weapons, once fully powered up, allow certain Aeons to break the damage limit.

Celestial Mirror

Requirements: Cloudy Mirror

After becoming Chocobo certified, go to Remiem Temple and race the champion chocobo. Winning for the first time will reward you with the Cloudy Mirror. Go to Macalania Woods and find a woman missing her husband. Find the husband and return to the wife to find their son is now missing. Continue talking with either parent until they express concern, then travel the crystal walkways until you find the son in front of an altar. This altar will transform the Cloudy Mirror into the Celestial Mirror.
